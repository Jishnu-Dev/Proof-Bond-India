What is Proof Bond?

Proof Bond is your ultimate solution,Eco friendly, Non polluting, Unique elastomeric polymer coating. It resists moisture, algae & fungus and provides durable,flexible & UV Resistant coating.It is developed under the most modern German technology which has revolutionized the water proofing industry in Germany, Italy, France, Scotland etc.
